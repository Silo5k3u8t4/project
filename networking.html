Networking - Future Scope after Diploma in Computer Engineering

Scope of Networking

Networking involves designing, implementing, managing, and troubleshooting computer networks. It includes tasks such as configuring routers and switches, setting up security protocols, and ensuring network performance.

Salary in Networking

The salary in the field of networking varies based on factors such as experience, location, and job role. On average, network administrators can earn around $70,000 per year, while network engineers may earn over $90,000 per year.

Certification Courses

  • Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)
  • Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP)
  • CompTIA Network+
  • Juniper Networks Certified Internet Associate (JNCIA)
  • Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate

Potential Positions

  • Network Administrator
  • Network Engineer
  • Network Security Analyst

  • Network Architect
